Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Timestamp Calculator Bot (the "Bot") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Discord bot service.

2. Information We Collect

The Bot collects minimal information necessary to provide its services:

  • Command Data: Date, time, and country information you provide when using the /timestamp command
  • Discord User ID: Temporarily stored to manage button interactions (deleted after 15 minutes)
  • Technical Data: Discord server IDs and channel IDs where commands are executed (for proper functionality)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Calculate and display timestamps based on your input
  • Enable interactive button functionality for 15 minutes after command execution
  • Ensure proper delivery of responses to your commands

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Temporary Storage: Your Discord User ID and timestamp data are stored in memory only for 15 minutes to support button interactions, then automatically deleted
  • No Long-term Storage: We do not store any personal information, command history, or usage data in any database
  • No Logs: We do not maintain logs of user commands or personal information

5. Information Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with third parties. The Bot operates independently and does not transmit your data to any external services except as required by Discord's API to function.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the temporary data processed by the Bot. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Stop using the Bot at any time
  • Request information about what data (if any) we have about you
  • Know that all your interaction data is automatically deleted after 15 minutes

8. Children's Privacy

The Bot does not knowingly collect information from users under 13 years of age. If you are under 13, please do not use the Bot. Discord's Terms of Service require users to be at least 13 years old.

9. Third-Party Services

The Bot operates on Discord's platform and is subject to Discord's Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review Discord's privacy practices.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the Bot after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Data Processing Location

The Bot processes data on servers provided by Replit. All data processing is temporary and in-memory only.

12. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the Bot's data practices, please contact us through the Bot's support server or direct message.

13. Compliance

This Bot is designed to be compliant with Discord's Developer Terms of Service and Developer Policy. We are committed to protecting user privacy and operating transparently.
